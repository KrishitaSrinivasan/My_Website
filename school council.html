My main goal has always been to bring the point of view of my classmates to the people who can make a change and solve their problems. Each year I have tried to do something unique, and to make the school a place where people want to come to. For example, in year seven I suggested to add an additional reward in the form of a raffle for those people with clean uniform cards (which means they followed all the uniform regulations). This gave one person, who had a clean uniform card, to have a chance in winning a 25 gift voucher. In year8 I suggested to have a culture day known as "the world fair" where people dress up and display their cultures, celebrating how diverse the world is! All of my suggestions have been approved and have been put in place, the world fair is now an annual tradition for the high school and the raffle takes place at the end of every big term.
